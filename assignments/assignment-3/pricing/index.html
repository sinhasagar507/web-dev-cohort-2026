Frequently asked questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate any billing adjustments.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees or hidden costs. You only pay for your chosen plan, and you can start with our free tier.

Can I try Pro features for free?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of our Pro plan with no credit card required. You can explore all features risk-free.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits. You can upgrade your plan or we'll help you find the best solution for your needs.
